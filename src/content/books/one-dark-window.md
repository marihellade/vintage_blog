I am 144 pages in and NOTHING has happened. The nightmare in her head is childish. The little riddles and poems are not well done. The 'Gothic'  atmosphere is barely there. Just because there is mist/fog doesn't make it a Gothic novel. The fact that at this point in the book we only know there are cards to collect and nothing else really is just driving me insane. The side characters have barely been developed at this point and it's so painfully obvious who the love interest is right from when they look at each other that I can't deal with it. The insta love just really put me off even further. The main character is so bland and woe is me that I just don't think I can carry on.
