Red Rising is a different energy from my usual reads — rawer, angrier, faster. Darrow's story is propulsive in a way that makes you forget to eat dinner. The world is brutal and the stakes are enormous, but there's a beating heart underneath all the violence.

Still working through this series. It's the kind of book where you finish a chapter and realise your jaw has been clenched for twenty pages.
