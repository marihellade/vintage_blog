Shield of Sparrows has the kind of warmth that sneaks up on you. It's not the loudest book on the shelf, but there's a quiet stubbornness to the characters that kept pulling me forward. Found family stories get me every time, and this one delivers it with just enough peril to keep things interesting.

A good curl-up-on-the-couch read.
