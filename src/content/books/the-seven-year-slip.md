This was a surpirse favourite for me. I read the first few pages and then atually set it aside and wasn't sure if I was going to continue as I felt the wriring style was off putting Fast foward it's the New Year and I am on a train in Japan and didn't have anything else to read so I continued. What followed was a wholesome story following Clementine as she navigates her life after some life changing events. I think this was just the right timing as it focus its focus on travel, food and book, heartwarming characters and splash of magical realisim just hit the rigth spot. This story made me want to paint my own book margins. Really enjoyable read during my holiday and into the new year. Authors note made me appreciate it even more. 4.5 Stars. I would like to get my own copy!
