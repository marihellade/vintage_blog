 This was a surprise favourite for me. I read the first few pages and then actually set it aside and wasn't sure if I was going to continue as I felt the writing style was off putting Fast forward it's the New Year and I am on a train in Japan and didn't have anything else to read so I continued. What followed was a wholesome story following Clementine as she navigates her life after some life changing events. I think this was just the right timing as it focus its focus on travel, food, books, heartwarming characters and splash of magical realism that just hit the right spot. This story made me want to paint my own book margins. Really enjoyable read during my holiday and into the new year. Authors note made me appreciate it even more. 4.5 Stars. I would like to get my own copy!
